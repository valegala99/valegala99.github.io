encoded invitation

Recorded, composed, mixed and mastered by Valerio Galadini between 2022 and 2024, encoded invitation is meant to be an invitation to interface with a virtual city that exists in a virtual reality context, where people live in a likely near future, if not the present.

Through eight tracks, meant to represent places belonging to this urban environment, different experiences are explored within a vivid and richly evocative sonic world, where every detail has its own place and function.

Intricate electronic sonorities, ambient recordings, found objects and voices are the elements that make up the sonic palette used, which bring narrative and scenography to life, embellished by a synthesis of aesthetic tropes and a collection of technological objects. The invitation is to close your eyes and visit this sound city.
