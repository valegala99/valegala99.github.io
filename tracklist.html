Tracklist

invitation…   
Ext. city, daytime, cloudy. Vehicles of various types, distant speeches, parking sensor, sirens, noisy vehicle approaching, melodic sounds and drones from stores, announcement, cell phone notifications. Int. shopping center. Air fan, more stores, elevator, electric bird sounding device, more notifications. Then ext. city again, daytime, cloudy. Distant cuckoos, melodic element increasingly expanding, streetcars, phone calls and voices, set of electromechanical sounds, water like it’s raining. Ext. silent street, nighttime. Car slowly driving away, people talking in a restaurant, sounds of dishes and glasses. Introduction showing the virtual city. Path through a busy street, inside a shopping center, then back on the street, various dialogues of phone talking, then coda in a silent place. Excitement of living in a dynamic city, sense of grandeur.   

save_as   
Int. bar/restaurant, nighttime. Plates, cutlery, glasses, electronic gadgets, cell phone, people talking, camera with time travelling power. Int. fancy event hall, nighttime. Photographers taking pictures of well-known people, glasses, laughter, slowing time gong. Representation of social life in a city venue with Asian elements of inner design. Contemplation of customers at tables. Opening and closing doors, moving to another room hosting a fancy event. Returning to the previous room, with a dialogue in the crowd in which the protagonist misunderstands the words of the other interlocutors. Moments of reflective emptiness, in which the venue is empty. Melodic elements that mimic moods and represent the aesthetics of the place.     

2 million years of rain   
Int. water-themed spa/hotel, dark room, nighttime. Bed, blue lights, aquarium, electronic aquariums that make bubble sounds, air conditioner, washing machines, electronic gadgets, hot tub. Int. bathroom, nighttime. Bathtub, shower, faucets, soap bubbles. Seabed. Marine turbine, colorful electronic fish, huge sea creature, bioluminescence. Contemplation, from lying down position, of the bedroom described above and the marine environment surrounding the transparent walls of the room. Call to a call center to purchase a product. Use of the new bathtub with female company. Immersion in the tub and teleportation to a seabed. Culmination in the seabed. Reflexivity, solitude, beauty. 

udenkiyo-e   Int. bowling alley. Adjacent bowling alleys, people playing, amusement arcade. Int. billiard room. Broken cables emitting electric sparks, people playing, device from which an unintelligible electric voice comes out, conversation with a friend. Moment among friends, selection of game mode, rapid succession of various video games, use of an arcade machine containing a new video game, final winning. Hyperactivity, overstimulation, nostalgia.  

anamnesi, session #1   
Int. therapist's office, evening. Rain on the glass, cuckoo, relaxing music in the background, swinging pendulum/metronome. Therapy with a physician, who has ordered the letters of the alphabet by frequency of use, for a patient who is unable to speak or move but can only communicate by blinking. Inner dialogue and attempt to express himself through sounds. The patient wants to die because of his current condition. Intention to “heal” the listener with a calmer piece. 
Dialogues: 
-Good morning, we will look for a way to communicate before you regain speech. We have sorted the letters according to the frequency of their use, you see? E, S, A, R, I, N, T, U, L, O, M, D, P, C, F, P, C … 
-I will recite this alphabet very slowly, one letter after another. You think what you want to say to me, and blink when you are ready. When the word is complete, close it twice as if pressing the space key on a keyboard. Do you want to tell me something, do you feel like it? Then I begin. 
-List of letters. 
-I know it's hard, let's go slowly. List of letters 
-List of letters, anything? Letter lists 
-As you see it's not the same alphabet you learn in school, E it's not the first letter 
-List of letters, die… you want to die. 
-O, O, I want. I want to. 
-List of letters 
-Am I a man? Am I a woman then? Does the sunlight bother you? Is it midnight? 
-Overlapping lists of letters. 


voicemail   
Int. office, daytime. Various electronic devices, cuckoo, water dispenser, telephones, laughter among female colleagues, secretary. Depiction of an office in a skyscraper, flashback of an accident, view of the city and an imposing blue skyscraper opposite, tape rewinding to rewind memories, dialogues between colleagues with gossip topics. Suspenseful sensation.    

-endless connection-   
Ext. outdoor workstation facing a park, daytime, sunny. Computer, traffic, birds, keyboard and mouse sounds, assembly line and mechanical arm, fountain, cicadas. Ext. workstation in the park, daytime, sunny. Birds, computer, keyboard sounds, fountain, pond, wind, grass, leaves, electronic devices, electronic uploads, synthetic birds, large technological installations. Int. greenhouse, workstation extension, daytime, cloudy. Mounting chain and mechanical arm, plant irrigation system, computer, large-scale technological installations. Representation of a park, firstly viewed from a workstation in front, then inside, then again from the first location. Artificial nature, generation of synthetic birds mingling with those in the park, and then also natural elements. Triangular speech between three people, with different points of view, on the subject of human existence. Melodic elements in the sky representing the infinite echoes of our existence. Sensation of conclusion and eternity. 
Triangular speech: 
-What do you desire? 
-What do you want to achieve? 
-What do you seek? 
-The current self 
-People around your current self 
-The environment surrounding your current self 
-None of this is meant to last forever 
-Your time is continuously flowing 
-And your world consists of the succession of relative changes 
-In reality we are all the same 
-This causes fear 
-This causes anxiety 
-This is why we feel loneliness 
-This is why we want to unite our souls 
-We want to become one 
-This is why we now seek to become one 
-We are trying to fill each other 
-Such is the plan for perfection 
-Why do we live? 
-Maybe I live precisely to know why 
-Because by thinking this way and doing nothing, you can avoid even hurting yourself 
-Who do you live for? 
-Of course I live for myself 
-I only want to do pleasant things 
-Are you happy in living? 
-Of course I am happy! 
-Are you happy in living? 
-I don't like it 
-The speeches overlap 
-After all, I don't have anything.   

walkthrough   
Ext. busy street, afternoon, cloudy. Crowds, cameras, melodic sounds and drones from stores, bar/restaurant with plates and glasses, distant announcement. 
Ext. park, afternoon, cloudy. Cars opposite, birds, footsteps on plants and gravel, melodic motif from '-endless connection-'. 
Ext. deserted street, evening, dark, pouring rain. Rain on umbrella, footsteps in puddles, toy device that sounds like an electric bird, place of 'anamnesis, session #1' in front, cuckoo. 
Int. reverberating dark gallery, evening. Sliding doors, few people walking, distant rain, electric sounds, cell phone, udenkiyo-e environment in distance. 
Int. bowling alley, evening. Crowd, skittles, video games. Ext. viewpoint, evening, cloudy. Wide view of entire city, shouting in the distance, vehicles in distance, voicemail skyscraper in front. A soundwalk in the virtual city, to get a broader view and relive the moments experienced in some of the places in the previous tracks. Descriptive approach, final calm moment, death of music after a last chordal breath.
